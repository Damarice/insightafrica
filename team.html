Our Leadership

Meet The Visionary Minds Behind Insight Pathways East Africa

Our diverse team of experts brings together decades of experience across education, migration, health, and community development to create impactful solutions for Eastern Africa's most pressing challenges.

Denish Odanga, PhD

Executive Director & Founder

As the Executive Director and founder of Insight Pathways East Africa, Dr. Odanga provides strategic leadership, vision, and oversight to guide the organization's mission and long-term impact. He leads on program design, research direction, external engagement, and cross-pillar integration, ensuring that work remains grounded in academic rigor, policy relevance, and community realities.

With a PhD in Cultural Studies from the University of Potsdam, Germany and a master's in education arts from Jaramogi Oginga Odinga University of Science and Technology, Kenya, Dr. Odanga brings extensive research experience in education, forced migration, teacher and learner well-being, socio-cultural trauma, and radicalization. His career spans teaching across multiple curricula including the 8.4.4 system in Kenya, IB and IGCSE in Germany, and the American K-12, as well as university-level teaching and supervision.

Education Policy Forced Migration Cultural Studies Teacher Development

Zacharia Abshir

Managing Director, Operations

Zacharia Abshir oversees the organization's operations, internal systems, and program execution. Working closely with program leads and specialized teams, he ensures smooth implementation across our three pillars. With a strong background in organizational management and program delivery, Abshir ensures that projects remain efficient, responsive, and aligned with community needs.

Responsible for team coordination, operational planning, financial oversight, and overall progress tracking, Abshir brings extensive experience in research and business operations from across the Horn of Africa, making him a vital asset to Insight Pathways' organizational effectiveness and community impact.

Organizational Management Program Implementation Horn of Africa Strategic Planning

Kevin Magero

Programs & Partnerships Consultant

Kevin Magero serves as a strategic advisor to Insight Pathways East Africa, supporting the growth and impact of our programs through a collaborative, results-oriented approach. His expertise spans resource mobilization, program strategy & design, monitoring & evaluation, and programmatic logistics & delivery.

Kevin advises on new grant and funding opportunities aligned with our mission, supports the development of high-quality proposals, and provides input into program conceptualization and design. He also contributes to the development of M&E frameworks and ensures that data collection processes are ethical, efficient, and useful for learning and impact reporting.
