Our Strategic Units

Explore the key strategic units driving our mission and their core functions across Education & Inclusion, Migration & Displacement, and Health & Wellbeing.

Research, Data & Learning Hub

Core Function: Generates insights into design, policy, and learning

  • Education & Inclusion: Studies inclusive education models, access gaps, and teacher capacity
  • Migration & Displacement: Produces research on migration trends, displacement drivers, and integration models
  • Health & Wellbeing: Collects data on community health, trauma support, and maternal health

Monitoring, Evaluation & Impact (MEI)

Core Function: Tracks performance and measures impact

  • Education & Inclusion: Assesses learning outcomes, gender inclusion, and vocational success
  • Migration & Displacement: Monitors program results in resettlement, legal access, and migration outcomes
  • Health & Wellbeing: Evaluates mental health, reproductive care, and WASH effectiveness

Communications & Advocacy

Core Function: Drives storytelling, campaigns, and public engagement

  • Education & Inclusion: Highlights learner voices, gender equity, and inclusive practices
  • Migration & Displacement: Amplifies stories from migrants, refugees, and displaced youth
  • Health & Wellbeing: Raises awareness on health rights, psychosocial needs, and adolescent care

Partnerships & Localization

Core Function: Builds community-led collaborations and local networks

  • Education & Inclusion: Partners with local schools, disability orgs, and community educators
  • Migration & Displacement: Engages diaspora groups, refugee-led orgs, and grassroots mobility partners
  • Health & Wellbeing: Works with local clinics, traditional healers, and health workers

Operations & Administration

Core Function: Manages logistics, finance, HR, compliance, and risk

  • Education & Inclusion: Supports safe learning hubs, inclusive materials, and program delivery
  • Migration & Displacement: Oversees logistics in displacement zones and cross-border operations
  • Health & Wellbeing: Manages medical supply chains, mobile health staffing, and operational risk

Advancing access to quality education, holistic health, and dignified migration solutions for marginalized communities.
